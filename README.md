## ðŸ§» Infinite Toilet Paper Roll

A completely useless but oddly satisfying website where you can pull down a 3D toilet paper roll infinitely!

### Features
- **3D Rendered Toilet Paper Roll**: Beautiful canvas-based 3D visualization
- **Interactive Pulling**: Click/touch and drag down to pull the paper
- **Infinite Paper**: The paper extends forever... because why not?
- **Distance Tracker**: See how many meters of paper you've wasted
- **Smooth Animation**: Watch the roll gently rotate when idle

### How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click/touch and hold on the toilet paper
3. Drag downward to pull the paper
4. Keep pulling... it never ends!
